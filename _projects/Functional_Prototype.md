The purpose of the prototype was to design a vibrating column that demonstrated the final project's intent of a zapping mechanism that vibrates at 60 Hz to attract spotted lanternflies.

We tested three main aspects:
1) Vibration Frequency: whether the vibration is transmitted along the full length of the rod
2) Vibration Consistency: consistency of vibration over time
3) Structural Stability: ability of the rod to remain upright under applied force

What the outcome was:
1) The column successfully vibrated at 25 Hz (kept lower than 60 Hz due to weaker motor)
2) Vibrations were consistent over 5 minute period
3) Vibration was detectable by touch
4) The structure remained stable under 5N of force
